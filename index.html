Play What You Do Supporting Solution Output for the Client
AI Visibility SnapshotTop of funnel Run an initial benchmark showing whether the SMB appears in AI answers, where competitors appear, and what content or trust signals are missing. AEO visibility score, competitor comparison, brand mentions, AI traffic insights, client-ready snapshot reports. Simple report showing current AI visibility, competitor gaps, and next actions.
AEO AuditDiagnose Diagnose the site's AI-readiness across structure, schema, content depth, local signals, FAQs, reviews, and technical crawlability. AEO dashboard, crawler monitoring, visibility metrics, recommendations, schema support, site audit workflows. Prioritized action plan for improving AI visibility.
AI-Ready Website MigrationProject Move clients from legacy CMSs, weak builders, or outdated sites into a site structured for AI discovery. Modern site builder, performance foundations, server-side rendering, schema, dynamic pages, templates, local integrations. Rebuilt site that is faster, more structured, easier to manage, and better prepared for AI crawlers.
Technical AEO FixesTechnical Clean up site structure, improve semantic clarity, add schema, improve page hierarchy, optimize internal linking, and make business facts machine-readable. Local schema, FAQ schema, product/service schema, SEO controls, structured content, dynamic pages. Site that gives AI clearer answers about who the business is, what it offers, where it operates, and why it should be trusted.
Local AEO PackageLocal Sync and clean up local business data, improve GBP consistency, add local schema, build service-area content, and expand location-specific pages. Google Business Profile sync, local listings, local schema, dynamic pages, reviews, connected data. Stronger local presence for AI engines, maps, and local-intent searches.
Content Expansion ProgramContent Build and refresh content around real customer questions: blogs, FAQs, comparison pages, service pages, pricing pages, and location pages. Blog tools, AI blog generation, blog optimization agent, FAQ agent, dynamic pages, content scheduling. Fresh, structured, answer-first content designed to be cited by AI engines.
FAQ / Answer OptimizationContent Turn key pages into direct-answer pages with structured FAQs, question-based headings, short answer blocks, and schema. FAQ agent, FAQ schema, AI content tools, page editor, structured content blocks. Pages that are easier for AI systems to parse, quote, and recommend.
Presence OptimizationPresence Improve trust signals by aligning listings, reviews, business facts, sentiment, and external validation. GBP sync, local listings, review widgets, sentiment tracking, brand monitoring, competitor tracking. More consistent and trustworthy online presence across AI-readable sources.
Competitive IntelligenceInsight Track where competitors appear, which prompts they win, which topics they dominate, and where the client is missing. Competitor configuration, prompt tracking, AI model tracking, geo customization, visibility comparisons. Ongoing competitor movement report and opportunity backlog.
AEO Reporting RetainerRecurring Send recurring reports showing visibility gains, citation changes, AI traffic, competitor movement, and lead/conversion impact. Client-ready PDF snapshots, AEO dashboard, traffic from AI sources, brand mentions, conversions, API access. Monthly proof of value that keeps clients engaged after launch.
Agentic AEO OperationsAutomate Use AI agents or automated workflows to analyze AEO data, generate content briefs, refresh stale pages, produce reports, and flag opportunities. AEO API, AEO data, blog optimization agent, FAQ agent, agent-ready workflows. Scaled AEO execution without adding equivalent headcount.
Portfolio ExpansionGrow Run AEO audits across the existing customer base, segment accounts by visibility gaps and commercial value, and trigger targeted upsell campaigns. Multi-site management, AEO dashboard, reporting snapshots, API, automation workflows. Prioritized list of current clients ready for migration, content, local AEO, or monitoring upsells.
Table 03

What to Say

Showing 12 of 12 messages
Client Situation What to Say Why It Works Proof Point / Deliverable
Prospect doesn't know what AEO is "Search is changing. Customers are no longer only clicking blue links. They are asking AI who to trust, who is nearby, and who to call. The question is whether AI can understand and recommend your business." Educates without jargon. Creates urgency without fearmongering. Snapshot showing current visibility, competitors, mentions, and gaps.
Prospect thinks SEO is enough "Ranking on Google still matters, but it is no longer the whole game. AI engines choose a small set of businesses to recommend. We need to make sure your website, content, reviews, and business data are clear enough to be selected." Repositions AEO as an expansion of SEO, not a replacement. Visibility score, competitor comparison, crawler activity, citation gaps.
Prospect has an old website "Your current site may look fine to a person, but AI systems need structure: clear services, locations, schema, FAQs, reviews, and fresh content. If AI cannot read it, it may ignore it." Creates a practical reason for migration beyond design taste. Rebuilt site with schema, dynamic pages, GBP sync, FAQs, and monitoring.
Prospect has low lead volume "AI visibility is becoming a lead-generation channel. If your business is cited or recommended in AI answers, those visitors can be more qualified because AI has already helped them narrow the options." Connects AEO to revenue, not vanity metrics. AI traffic, form submissions, click-to-call reporting, conversion tracking.
Local SMB wants more calls "When someone asks AI for the best provider nearby, the model looks for clear location, services, reviews, hours, and trust signals. We will make those signals easier to find and verify." Makes local AEO concrete and actionable. GBP sync, local schema, listings, reviews, service-area pages.
SMB has thin content "AI needs specific answers. A five-page website usually does not give it enough information. We need pages that answer real customer questions by service, location, price, urgency, and use case." Explains why content depth matters without sounding like generic blogging. Blog posts, FAQs, service pages, location pages, structured answer blocks.
SMB has inconsistent listings or reviews "AI engines do not only read your website. They compare your business facts across the web. If your listings, reviews, hours, or service details are inconsistent, trust goes down." Ties reputation and listings work directly to AI visibility. Listings cleanup, GBP sync, review widgets, sentiment tracking.
Existing client asks why they need ongoing work "AI visibility is not a one-time fix. Competitors change, AI models change, content gets stale, and customer questions evolve. We need to monitor and improve this continuously." Justifies retainer after the initial build. Monthly visibility snapshot, citation movement, competitor movement, recommendations.
Client wants proof "We will not just say we improved your visibility. We will show AI mentions, crawler activity, competitor comparisons, traffic from AI sources, and business outcomes like leads and calls." Moves the discussion from effort to measurable outcomes. Client-ready PDF snapshot and dashboard reporting.
Client is worried about complexity "You do not need to understand every AI model. That is our job. Your job is to run the business. Our job is to make sure AI can understand, trust, and recommend it." Positions you as the trusted advisor. Audit, implementation, monitoring, content, reporting — handled end-to-end.
Client is comparing partners "Most providers are still selling SEO as rankings and traffic. We are building your visibility system for how customers actually discover businesses now: search, AI answers, maps, reviews, and direct recommendations." Differentiates you from generic SEO vendors. Modern site, content engine, local presence, monitoring, reporting.
Client asks how this scales "We have the infrastructure to manage this at scale: structured websites, local business data, dynamic pages, schema, content tools, AI visibility reporting, and automation through APIs." Explains the platform advantage in business terms. AI-ready site plus AEO dashboard, reports, and optimization workflows.
